BingoBongoTesting

Introduction

BingoBongo testing is a simple but efficient, explorative test methodology to find issues and having fun.

Preparation

BingoBongo works best if you focus on an specific part or feature of your application.
Simply adapt this template to your test case.
Invite the testers (4-8 persons) and make sure everybody has access to the application and is able to share the screen.

The Meeting

Every BingoBongo meetings strictly follows the same rules.
  • Initial presentation done by the moderator
    • The Goal
    • The Rules
    • Introduction / Demo of the feature to test
    • Known Issues & Test focus
  • The Game
    • Everybody test on his own
    • If you find something - shout ' BingoBongo’
    • Everyone stops working; the Finder shares his screen and presents the finding
    • If everyone agrees, the Finder has to fill an issue in the bugtracker and gets a point
    • repeat
  • The Winner is
    • Have a final look at the results and congratulation.
